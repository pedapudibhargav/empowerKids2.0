Our Mission

EmpowerKids Foundation's mission is to empower children in developing countries through education. We are committed to supporting small communities in developing countries by creating supportive environments for learning. We build infrastructures that will allow children to learn and develop without being impeded by economic hardships.

Vivek Kumar

Vivek is a firm believer in the power of education as a tool to empower children.
